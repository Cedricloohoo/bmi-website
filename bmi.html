BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight based on height and weight.

BMI Formula

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m)

How to Calculate BMI

1. Open WhatsApp
2️. Send a message like:
Height: 175 cm
Weight: 70 kg
3. Receive your BMI instantly

Why WhatsApp?

This project uses automation to make BMI calculation faster and easier without filling forms.
